Why Conserve Wildlife?

Wildlife plays a vital role in maintaining ecological balance. By conserving species like tigers, rhinos, and elephants, we protect the very ecosystems that support all life on Earth.

The Plight of the Tiger

Tigers are on the brink due to habitat destruction and poaching. Through protection laws, reserves, and education, we can reverse their decline and ensure their future.

Endangered Birds & Ecosystem Fragility

Birds like the spoonbill and great Indian bustard are key indicators of healthy habitats. Their loss signals wider ecosystem stress that can impact all species, including humans.
